No 1 About Project

This is a single player third person view shooter game against AI enemies on an artic style map.

The player needs to eliminate all enemy robots within the building before own health down to zero. AI enemies gonna keep chasing you once they spot your location. If they lose sight of you, they gonna try to find you by going to where they saw you the last time. If they still cannot find you, they will go back to their own spawn and waiting for you to appear. So the key is to play smart and avoid AI's sight using props in the map as cover, so that you are not facing multiple enemies at the same time.

No 3 Features

— Particle effect

Added several auto-switchable particle effects on both the muzzle and the hit point where impacts occur.

— Input binding

Binded player input with character movement via PlayerInputComponent in c++. Both Keyboard and Controller supported.

— Aiming Mechanism

Adjusted third person camera spring arm to set a proper aimmig view. Let characters hold the gun with their eye on the scope by attaching to meshes via sockets. So that it feels like the character is aiming at the players viewpoint, where I also put a crosshair. Utilized line trace by channel to generate hit result. Ignored certain actors in line traces to avoid shooting chracter itself and its own gun mesh.

— Animation

Adopted Blend Space and Animation Graph Blueprint to combine animations of characters with their movement and actions. Adjusted transforming vectors and calculated animation speed to make it perfectly fit and make animation looks smooth no matter which angle we are looking or moving to.

— AI

Set up AI controller to make them focus at the player. Utilized nav mesh and AI movement to make bots follow us. Leveraged BehaviorTrees and Blackboard keys to control AI's behavior as we wish. Also used techniques of BT Decorators and selectors, c++ customized BTTasks and BTServices to make AI complete more complex tasks.

— Sound

Added background music, shooting and impact sound effects. Added randomized sound cues to make it sounds more real. Activated sound Spatialization which allow players to better judge enemies location.

— State Machine

Encapsulated animations with state machine, set states like grounded, airborne,alive and death, etc. Looks nice and clean.

— Win&Lose Screen, Crosshair and HUD

Put a health bar which hooked up with my damage system as HUD. Used Widget Blueprint to show win or lose screen as well as our crosshair.
